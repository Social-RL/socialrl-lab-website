Contact for Prospective Lab Members


Thank you for your interest in our lab! We are always looking for strong Ph.D students, postdocs and undergraduate students to join our lab.

Prospective PhD Students

If you are interested in joining the lab as a PhD student, please apply to the UW CSE PhD program through the normal process, and list Natasha Jaques as a potential advisor in your application. Dr. Jaques and others will review your application, and contact you if you are a good fit for the lab. Please do not contact us directly with regard to PhD admissions, as we will not be able to reply to emails from individual applicants.

Prospective Postdocs

If you are interested in a post-doc position, please reach out to Dr. Jaques at nj@cs.washington.edu. Include a link to your google scholar, your CV, your desired start date, and a 1-2 sentence intro highlighting your best papers or credentials. Because of the large volume of emails I receive, I may not be able to respond to all applicants.

Prospective Undergraduate or MS Students

We do not hire undergrads from institutions other than the University of Washington. If you are a UW student, please fill out this application form.
